Trip to St. Louis

Friday March 17, 2023

At the time I’m typing this, I’m already in St. Louis. It’s surprising that we stayed on the 10th floor. It’s the highest floor in this building.

The way there was ok. For most of the trip, It didn’t feel much different from a normal trip to Conway but when we arrived to St. Louis, many tall buildings were towering upon Us. Below are some pictures of them.

When we got there, the building was very historic. They first opened in 1903 and they never remodeled much at all.

It may be blurry but it’s the camera’s problem with auto blurring and 2-second image buffering.

 

We got rooms at the 10th floor and I’m seeing a great view. Its 9:30 now and St. Louis is really beautiful.
