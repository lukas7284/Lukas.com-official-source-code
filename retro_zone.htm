my journey through office 95-2010:

recently, i installed every version of office upgrading from one to the other.

I did not take a capture of office 95. but it does feel period accurate.

Microsoft office 97.

"office 97 never removed anything, they only added".

-me.

it wasn't that much of a deal that i never took that capture since 97 is not that much different when it comes with ui.

clippy was added. this office assistant is in the bottom right corner. and was known for being "annoying". but i never saw much annoyance.

office 2000:

this version changed the layout in some ways. there is only 2 rows instead of 3 rows of options.

clippy was redesigned and i noticed clippy being annoying now because clippy just tries to immediately talk for the littlest things.

but a lot of new features were added.

 

office xp (or 2002)

there is not much to this that's different so im going to move on.

 

office 2003

 

this is the first version and the last version that has different things.

they have completely changed the ui now. but is the last version with clippy. this is the first version that included publisher. before, office publisher was not included in Microsoft office. now it is.

 

office 2007

 

 

office 2007 is a end of an era. the ribbons style is here and will continue on to the latest version.

this is the first and last version with a background as you can see.

 

you might not be able to see the text very good. this is because the resolution needs to be bigger since having a smaller resolution would make office 2010 display wrong. the storage requirements are getting bigger as well. requiring 2 GB of data.

it just felt to heavyweight to feel comfortable.
